# sales-dashboard-excel
# Overview
This project presents an interactive Sales Dashboard developed to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, product ratings, and profit margins. The dashboard provides key business insights that help identify top-performing regions, products, categories, and sales representatives.

# Key Insights

# Revenue by Region
South region generated the highest revenue (₹3,50,000).
West region generated the lowest revenue (₹90,000).
# Top Performing Sales Person
Ravi achieved the highest sales of ₹3,50,000.
Priya and Aman followed with strong performances.

# Product Performance
Products with high sales but lower ratings were identified:
Shoes
Watch
T-shirt

These products may require quality improvements to increase customer satisfaction.

# Revenue Trend Analysis

The dashboard tracks revenue trends across multiple dates and highlights sales fluctuations over time.

# Profit Analysis
Electronics contributed the highest profit percentage (63.09%).
Electronics also had the lowest profit margin (1.31%), indicating potential pricing or cost optimization opportunities.
